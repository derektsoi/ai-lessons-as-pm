# Gemini Prompt Template for Cover Images

## Base Prompt

```
Generate a 16:9 blog cover image for a Substack newsletter.
Warm cream/beige paper texture background.
Bold black sans-serif text reading "[MAIN TEXT]" large and centered.
Below in smaller black text: "[SUBTITLE]".
Small subtle text at the top left corner: "AI Lessons as a PM" in muted gray.
On the right side, a simple hand-drawn illustration of [METAPHOR DESCRIPTION].
Warm earth tones, cream and light brown palette.
Editorial magazine style. Clean, professional, minimalist.
No dark backgrounds.
```

## Common Refinement Prompts

**If text is wrong:**
> Regenerate with the exact text "[CORRECT TEXT]". Do not add or remove any words.

**If illustration feels precarious/unstable:**
> Make the [element] look more like deliberate architectural construction — uniform flat layers, not like Jenga pieces. It should feel intentional and solid.

**If figure is too small:**
> Make the figure on top slightly more prominent and visible — it needs to read clearly even at small thumbnail size.

**If no AI topic signal:**
> Add small subtle text at the top left corner that says "AI Lessons as a PM" in a muted gray or light brown color, like a newsletter series label.
